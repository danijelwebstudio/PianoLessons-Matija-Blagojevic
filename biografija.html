Matija Blagojević

Biography

Professional pianist and music educator based in New York. Below is Matija’s full professional biography, performance highlights, teaching approach and contact information.

Matija Blagojević is a New York-based professional pianist and music educator known for his dynamic performance style and passionate teaching approach. With roots in both classical and contemporary music, Matija has built a versatile career that spans solo performances, ensemble work, and private instruction.

He began studying piano at the age of six, quickly gaining recognition in his native country before moving to the United States to continue his musical education. Matija earned his degree from a prestigious conservatory, where he studied under renowned pianists and composers who helped shape his distinctive sound.

Throughout his career, Matija has performed in concert halls across North America, Europe, and Asia, captivating audiences with his expressive interpretation and technical precision. His repertoire includes works by classical masters such as Chopin, Beethoven, and Rachmaninoff, as well as modern and jazz compositions.

In addition to performing, Matija is deeply committed to music education. He offers private piano lessons to students of all ages and levels, focusing on developing strong foundational skills while nurturing each student’s individual creativity and musicality.

His lessons are carefully tailored to each student's goals, whether they are preparing for auditions, building a repertoire, or simply learning for personal enrichment. Matija combines traditional methods with modern tools, including online lessons for students outside New York.

He believes that music has the power to transform lives and serves as both an emotional outlet and a form of discipline. His students appreciate his patient, inspiring teaching style and the way he makes complex musical ideas approachable and fun.

Beyond teaching and performing, Matija continues to compose original music and collaborate with other musicians in the New York music scene. His work reflects a deep respect for tradition while embracing innovation and experimentation.

Matija is also an active member of several music education organizations and regularly participates in workshops, masterclasses, and community outreach programs. He is passionate about making music education accessible and engaging for everyone.
